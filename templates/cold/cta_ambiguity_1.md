Subject: {first_name}, your CTA is visible. They just don't know what it commits them to.

{first_name},

The audit flagged this: your CTA button is clear on what to click. But not on what happens after.

**What we saw:**

{cta_text}

"Get started" is visible. The question is: what follows?

- How much time?
- Do I have to talk to someone?
- Will I be charged?
- What do I actually receive?

The button is not a substitute for clarity. If the visitor has to guess the commitment, most won't click.

**Why it happens:**

Teams optimize for button color and placement, but skip the invisible work: defining the next step so the visitor doesn't have to infer it.

**One quick check:**

Look right below your CTA. Does it say what happens after the click? If not, you're asking them to trust you instead of showing them.
