# LinkedIn Welcome DM Templates

Daniel Bustamante's Welcome DM strategy: When someone connects, send a message that (1) thanks them AND (2) invites them to check out your best content.

Two approaches:
- **Lower ask**: Share 2-3 best LinkedIn posts (builds goodwill)
- **Higher conversion**: Share a link to your best lead magnet

---

## Version 1: Lower Ask (Goodwill First)

```
Hey [NAME],

Thanks for connecting.

Saw you're in [THEIR INDUSTRY/ROLE]. I've been writing about conversion issues that waste ad spend for founders like you.

Here are 3 posts that resonated this month:

1. "Why Your Landing Page Is Leaking Buyers (And How to Fix It in 15 Minutes)"
   → [LINK]

2. "The $147 Fix That Saved $3k/mo in Ad Spend"
   → [LINK]

3. "Stop Describing Your Product. Do This Instead."
   → [LINK]

If any of these hit home, I'd love to hear what you're working on.

Best,
Mike
```

---

## Version 2: Higher Conversion (Direct Offer)

```
Hey [NAME],

It takes 30 seconds, gives you a score, and shows exactly where you're bleeding money.

Want the link?

Best,
Mike
```


---

## Trigger Detection (for personalized DMs)

When accepting connection requests, check for:

| Signal | DM Variant |
|--------|------------|
| "Founder" or "CEO" in title | Version 1 or 2 |
| "Marketing" or "Growth" in title | Version 1 |
| "Ads" or "PPC" in recent posts | Version 2 (audit offer) |
| Shared connection in AdTech/SaaS | Version 3 (combined) |
| Generic profile (no clear signal) | Version 2 (safe default) |

---

## Compliance Notes

- Only automate if you have LinkedIn Premium (Sales Navigator can help)
- Manual DMs are safer for new connections
- Never send more than 50 DMs/day to avoid rate limits
- Always personalize the [NAME] field
