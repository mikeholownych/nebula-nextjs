## Headline — Outcome Specificity

**Finding:** Your headline doesn't clearly state the outcome a visitor gets.

**Your current headline:**
> ${headline_text}

**What's wrong:**
${headline_issue}

**The fix criteria:**
- Must name the specific outcome your visitor gets
- Must include your target audience (explicitly or by strong implication)
- Under 90 characters
- Must make someone in your audience think "that's for me"

### Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini:

```
I need a new headline for my landing page. Here's the context:

**Current headline:** "${headline_text}"
**Audience:** ${audience}
**What I offer:** ${offer}
**Page URL:** ${url}

The problem: ${headline_issue}

Write 5 alternative headlines that:
1. State a concrete outcome the visitor gets
2. Implicitly or explicitly identify the target audience
3. Are under 90 characters
4. Avoid vague claims — use specific results or mechanics

Format as a simple numbered list. No explanation. Just headlines.
```

**Expected output:** 5 headline options you can A/B test immediately. Pick the one that best matches your ad copy and swap it in.
