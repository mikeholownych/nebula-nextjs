# Nebula Discovery Template

## 1. Business Model Analysis

### Core Questions:
- What is your primary business model?
- Who are your main customers?
- What problem are you solving?
- What's your current revenue model?

### Data to Collect:
- Annual revenue
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Current marketing channels and spend
- Conversion rates at each funnel stage

## 2. Current Marketing Funnel Mapping

### Funnel Stages to Map:
1. **Awareness** - Where do your customers discover you?
2. **Interest** - How do they engage with your content?
3. **Consideration** - What convinces them you're the right choice?
4. **Conversion** - What's your current conversion path?
5. **Retention** - How do you keep customers engaged?

### Data Points:
- Traffic sources (organic, paid, referral)
- Landing page performance
- Lead generation metrics
- Sales conversion rates
- Customer retention rates

## 3. Technical Stack Assessment

### Key Areas:
- Website platform (WordPress, Shopify, custom)
- Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Conversion tracking (Facebook Pixel, Google Tag Manager)
- CRM system (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
- Email marketing platform

### Questions:
- How is your conversion data tracked?
- What tools integrate with your marketing efforts?
- Are there any technical limitations affecting your funnel?

## 4. Pain Point Identification

### Primary Pain Points:
- What's your biggest marketing challenge right now?
- Where are you losing customers in your funnel?
- What competitors are outperforming you and why?

### Data to Validate:
- Current conversion funnel visualization
- Heatmap data from user behavior tools
- Customer feedback (surveys, reviews)
- Competitor analysis data

## 5. Competitor Analysis

### Key Competitors to Analyze:
- Direct competitors in your niche
- Adjacent solutions that might be attracting your customers

### Analysis Framework:
- Value proposition comparison
- Pricing structure comparison
- Marketing channel effectiveness
- Customer experience differences
- Unique selling propositions

## 6. Stakeholder Interviews

### Interview Template (45-60 minutes per stakeholder)

**Founder/CEO:**
- Business vision and goals
- Current marketing challenges
- Budget constraints
- Success metrics

**Marketing Lead:**
- Current strategy and tactics
- Campaign performance data
- Team structure and resources
- Tools and platforms in use

**Technical Lead:**
- Technical constraints and capabilities
- Integration possibilities
- Implementation timeline considerations
- Data access and quality

## 7. Data Collection Plan

### Immediate Actions:
1. Request access to analytics dashboards
2. Set up temporary tracking for current campaigns
3. Schedule stakeholder interviews
4. Collect existing assets (copy, landing pages, etc.)

### Timeline:
- Day 1: Initial discovery call + access requests
- Day 2-3: Data collection and interviews
- Day 4: Analysis and synthesis
- Day 5: Discovery report delivery
