# Pilot Client Selection

## Selected Pilot Clients

Based on the score matrix analysis, we've selected the following 3 clients for our pilot program:

### 1. TechSolutions (SaaS) - Total Score: 7.95
**Rationale**: Highest budget availability ($210,000) and strong conversion numbers (780 conversions), indicating significant opportunity for improvement. SaaS industry is strategic for our long-term vision.

### 2. CloudServices (Cloud Infrastructure) - Total Score: 7.95  
**Rationale**: Large marketing spend ($175,000) with good conversion numbers (520 conversions). Cloud infrastructure is a growing market segment that aligns with our strategic direction.

### 3. Acme Corp (B2B Software) - Total Score: 7.95
**Rationale**: Established B2B client with proven relationship. Marketing spend of $125,000 with 420 conversions represents a solid opportunity for demonstrating framework effectiveness.

## Selection Justification

All three clients meet our core selection criteria:
- **Current Clients**: All are existing clients with active relationships
- **Medium Complexity**: Each has clear, measurable marketing challenges
- **Budget Availability**: All have confirmed budget for pilot implementation  
- **Strategic Fit**: Each aligns with our long-term vision and market focus
- **Implementation Readiness**: Each has technical and organizational capacity for framework adoption
