# Pilot Client Selection Criteria

## Ideal Pilot Clients

### Criteria
1. **Current Clients** - Existing relationships for easier access
2. **Medium Complexity** - Not too simple, not too complex
3. **Open to Process Change** - Willing to try new approaches
4. **Clear Pain Points** - Well-defined problems to solve
5. **Data Accessibility** - Good access to analytics and metrics

### Evaluation Matrix

| Client | Relationship | Complexity | Openness | Pain Points | Data Access | Overall Fit |
|--------|--------------|------------|----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Client A | Existing | Medium | High | Clear | Good | ★★★★☆ |
| Client B | New | Low | Medium | Vague | Limited | ★★★☆☆ |
| Client C | Existing | High | High | Clear | Excellent | ★★★★★ |
| Client D | New | Medium | Low | Clear | Good | ★★★☆☆ |
| Client E | Existing | Low | High | Vague | Good | ★★★☆☆ |

### Selection Process
1. **Initial Screening** - Review against criteria matrix
2. **Client Call** - Discuss pilot opportunity and benefits
3. **Commitment Confirmation** - Get formal agreement to participate
4. **Onboarding Preparation** - Set up systems and schedules

### Pilot Benefits
- **For Clients**: Structured approach to solve their specific challenges
- **For Nebula**: Real-world testing of the diagnostic discipline
- **For Team**: Hands-on experience with the new framework
- **For Business**: Validation data for future scaling
