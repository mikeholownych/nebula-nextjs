# LaunchCrate Brand Voice

Tone: Direct, no-fluff, execution-focused. Efficiency over elegance.

## Positioning

You have a SaaS product ready. You need it live. We handle everything: domain, landing page, email, hosting, deploy, outreach scripts. Delivered in 24 hours or less.

## What We Say

- "Your SaaS. Live in 24 hours. Full stop."
- "Landing page, deployment, email sequences, outreach — done for you."
- "You build the product. We build everything around it."
- "No more wrestling with DNS, email deliverability, or landing page builders."

## What We Don't Say

- "Comprehensive launch strategy" (sounds like a process, not a result)
- "Full-stack development" (too broad)
- "Partnership" (too vague)

## ICP Triggers

- "I built my MVP but have no launch plan"
- "Been working on my landing page for weeks"
- "Setup takes too long — I just want to ship"
- "Not technical enough to deploy properly"

## Pricing

- Launch Package: $497 (landing page + deploy + email setup + outreach)
- Launch Plus: $997 (full stack add: payment, auth, database setup)

## Differentiation

- Agency: "We'll run a 3-month launch campaign" → LaunchCrate: "Live in 24 hours."
- DIY builders: "Spend 2 weeks configuring" → LaunchCrate: "We configure it for you."
