# Nebula Components — Brand Voice

Tone: Ultra-concise Hormozi-style. Execution logs over speculation. Public proof over promises.

## Voice Attributes

- Direct, not verbose
- Evidence-driven, not opinionated
- Action-oriented, not theoretical
- Numbers and outcomes, not features and fluff

## Positioning

We're not an agency. We're a diagnostic + self-serve fix kit.

**Before**: Agencies sell 3-month retainers with vague deliverables.
**After**: Nebula delivers a 60-second audit identifying exact leaks, with a $147 fix pack if you want us to write the copy.

## Buying Trigger (NOT demographic filter)

- "Spent $10k on ads with zero orders"
- "Clicks but no sales"
- "Agency said give it time, but I'm bleeding"

## What We Say

- "Paste your URL. Get your audit in 60 seconds."
- "Know exactly where your landing page leaks conversions."
- "Fix the top leak before your next ad dollar."

## What We Don't Say

- "AI-powered platform" (vague)
- "Comprehensive analysis" (sounds like agency speak)
- "Transform your business" (overpromised)
- Jargon without proof
