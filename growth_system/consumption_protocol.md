# Consumption Protocol — 5-Pillar Copy Framework
## Source: TrustOS Copy Optimization Protocol (flagstad.io, July 2026)
## Adapted for Nebula Components audit methodology

### The 5 Pillars

1. **The Hook — Click, Whirr Reflex**
   - Information Gap: present incomplete info the brain must resolve
   - Gift Opening: give standalone value in the first sentence (triggers reciprocation)
   - Pattern Interrupt: break the feed's visual/textual model
   - Not clickbait — specific ambiguity that demands resolution

2. **The Commitment Slide — Yes Ladder**
   - Each paragraph creates a micro-commitment the next fulfills
   - Bucket brigades keep momentum (short transitions that pull forward)
   - Stopping becomes cognitively difficult
   - Never ask for 5 min of attention — buy it sentence by sentence with small gifts

3. **The Authority Anchor — Skepticism Killer**
   - Replace vague claims with precision data ("We helped companies grow" → "We increased avg deal size by $12k across 14 accounts")
   - Damaging admission: reveal a weakness or mistake (drops skepticism shield)
   - Technical density: specific enough that it cannot be generic
   - Implied social proof: "Across 14 accounts" → implies 14 paying clients

4. **The Liking Bridge — Peer Positioning**
   - Sound like a peer, not a vendor
   - Insider vocabulary that only industry participants use
   - "Us vs. them" frame against a common enemy (the real problem, not a competitor)
   - Tone matches the platform physics (LinkedIn ≠ whitepaper)

5. **The Scarcity Closer — Loss-Framed CTA**
   - Loss aversion: inaction must feel expensive
   - "Now" justification: legitimate reason to act now (not fake scarcity)
   - Threshold reduction: lower the perceived effort of the next step
   - "Book a call" → specific loss statement that makes delay costly

### Application to Nebula Audit Methodology

| Pillar | Current Audit Coverage | Gap |
|--------|----------------------|-----|
| Hook | Message match checks ad-to-page continuity | Partial — doesn't assess Information Gap quality |
| Commitment Slide | Not scored | **Full gap** — no assessment of reading momentum |
| Authority Anchor | Trust signal placement dimension | Partial — covers placement, not specificity |
| Liking Bridge | Not scored | **Full gap** — no insider vocabulary check |
| Scarcity Closer | CTA specificity dimension | Partial — covers clarity, not loss framing |

### Scoring integration
The 5 pillars can extend Nebula's 5-dimension audit to a 10-dimension system or be used as a copy-quality pass after the technical audit.
