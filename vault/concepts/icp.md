# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile
> NOT a demographic. A buying trigger: founder actively bleeding ad spend with zero conversions.

## The Trigger (what creates a qualified lead)
A founder who:
1. Is running paid ads (Google, Meta, TikTok)
2. Has a landing page that isn't converting (CVR < 2%, or CPA climbing)
3. Has eliminated the traffic problem and suspects the page
4. Is spending at least $500/mo on ads

## What they say
- "My ads are getting clicks but nobody's buying"
- "My CPA keeps going up and I don't know why"
- "My agency says add more budget but it's not working"
- "I've tested 3 headlines and nothing moved"

## What they don't say (but we can spot)
- Job post on Upwork: "Need a landing page CRO expert"
- Reddit/forum post: complaining about ad spend not converting
- LinkedIn: announcing ad campaign launch (predicts the pain in 30 days)

## ICP Anti-patterns (filter out)
- Founders shopping for a full rebrand
- People who want a call first
- Businesses without active ad spend
- Agencies reselling audits

## Channels where triggers fire
- Upwork job scraper (apify/neatrat~upwork-job-scraper)
- Reddit subreddits: r/PPC, r/Entrepreneur, r/startups
- LinkedIn signal: ad spend announcement → 30-day follow-up
- Cold email: personalized with the trigger artifact first
