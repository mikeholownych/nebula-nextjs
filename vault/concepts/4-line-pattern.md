# 4-Line Email Pattern

## Core Purpose
Every cold email must follow this exact structure:

```
Line 1: Relevance - why this buyer specifically (reference their post/product)
Line 2: Problem - the pain they demonstrated
Line 3: Offer - concrete solution, not vague
Line 4: CTA - clear next step
```

## Good Example
> "Saw your IH post about building for 2 months with zero users - that's exactly the problem I've been working on.
> I build landing pages and outreach sequences for founders stuck at zero. Delivered in 24 hours.
> Early adopter price: $97 (was $750).
> Want me to take a quick look at your page? Reply here."

## Bad Example (what NOT to send)
> "Hi there, I saw your post and thought you might like Nebula Components. It's a landing page kit on sale for $7. Check it out!"

## Implementation Rules
- **No pitch in the subject line** — "quick note on {domain}" not "buy now"
- **Teach ONE concept** (5-second test, message-match, above-fold CTA) — methodology first
- **Single CTA at the end** — implementation link only
- **Do NOT mention the audit score again** — they saw it; now give them the why
