# 3-Touch Follow-Up Cadence

## Core Purpose
Space touches 24-48h apart for optimal conversion

## Sequence Structure
1. **Touch 1**: Initial personalized outreach (product value prop)
2. **Touch 2 (24h later)**: Flash sale / limited-time discount with urgency
3. **Touch 3 (48h later)**: "Name Your Price" / final last-chance offer

## Rules
- **Do not send more than 3 touches**
- **Each touch should have a different angle and increasing urgency**
- **Do NOT send more than 3 touches** to the same lead

## Multi-Tier Upsell
After launching a higher-tier offer (e.g. DFY service), email all previous low-ticket leads about it. Frame it as a natural upgrade path and offer a one-time intro price for first X clients. Lead with the lower-tier success ("you saw the landing page quality — now let me build one for you").
