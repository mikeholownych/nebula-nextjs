# Free Audit First Funnel

## Core Principle
Sending payment links cold ($7 or $97) produces near-zero conversion. Cold audiences have no trust — asking for payment requires trust.

## Correct Cold Sequence
1. **Email offers a FREE audit/teardown/review** — zero friction ask
2. **Deliver a REAL, specific 3-point audit** (not generic "add social proof" advice — actual findings about *their* product)
3. **At the bottom of the audit email**: "If you want me to implement these fixes, it's $97 done-for-you in 24h — reply yes"
4. **Only send payment link AFTER trust is established via the free deliverable**

## Where to Find Warm Leads
- IndieHackers `/group/landing-page-feedback` — founders actively posting URLs asking for feedback
- HN Algolia: `search_by_date?query=landing+page+feedback&tags=ask_hn`
- Reddit r/roastmystartup
- These leads have *demonstrated need* — 10-20x better response than scraped cold lists

## What a Real Personalized Audit Looks Like
```
Hey Adam,

Read your IH thread and checked hard-book.com.

Three findings:
1. Your binding-contract reassurance is below the fold but the trust question fires before the CTA.
   Fix: Pull "Legally binding — same standing as DocuSign" up to sit directly under the first CTA.

2. The hero demo shows clickable buttons but a looping animation — broken UI signal in Chrome.
   Fix: Either kill the click targets or make the full demo interactive.

3. The 27-role carousel signals "for everyone" rather than "built specifically for me."
   Fix: Swap for a single "day in the life of a motion designer" mini-story.

If you want me to implement these — landing page rewrite, CTA section, trust hierarchy — it's $97 done-for-you in 24h. Reply yes.

— Mike
```
