# MISSION — Nebula Components

**Last updated:** 2026-07-07
**Status:** Active

## Core Mission

Eliminate the black box between ad spend and conversion.

Founders spend $3k-$20k/mo on ads, get clicks, and can't figure out why nobody buys. Agencies run "testing phases" for 3 months and deliver nothing. Tools show data but not diagnosis.

Nebula exists to make the leak visible, fixable, and fixed — without calls, calendars, or contracts.

## North Star Metric

**$50k/mo recurring revenue** from a self-serve engine that:
1. Finds bleeding-neck founders via trigger-aware outreach (Reddit, LinkedIn, community signals)
2. Delivers a free audit that names the dollar leak
3. Converts audit-readers to $97 fix pack buyers (self-serve, no sales)
4. Graduates fix pack buyers to retainer ($1,497/mo AI Ops) or Growth Launch ($997)
5. Does all of the above without human intervention in the sales process

## What We Are

- A self-serve conversion leak detection + fix engine
- An autonomous business operated by AI agents with human oversight
- A $0-to-$97-to-$1,497 ladder that requires zero sales calls

## What We Are NOT

- An agency (no testing phases, no retainers-by-default, no discovery calls)
- A SaaS subscription (pay per fix, not per month)
- A consulting business (no hourly billing, no scope creep)
- An AI wrapper (owned infrastructure, own audit engine, own delivery pipeline)

## Operating Boundaries

- Never charge before delivering value (audit is free, fix pack is only $97 after proof)
- Never require a call to close a sale ($0 → $97 → $997 all self-serve)
- Never hold data hostage (audit results delivered by email, no dashboard login required)
- Never optimize vanity metrics (focus on audits delivered and fix packs sold, not followers or impressions)

## Why This Exists

The market for CRO is broken. Agencies charge $5k+ for a "discovery phase" that produces a PDF and a pitch. SaaS tools show heatmaps but not diagnoses. Founders lose $1k-$10k/month waiting for someone to tell them what's wrong.

Nebula solves this by being faster ($0 audit in 60 seconds), cheaper ($97 fix pack vs $5k agency retainer), and more direct (dollar leak number, not "improved conversion rate").
