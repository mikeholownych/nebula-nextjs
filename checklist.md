# Nebula DIY Kit — 5-Step Implementation Checklist

Follow these steps to implement your audit findings and boost conversions:

1. **Fix the Hero Section**
   - If your CTA button says "Learn More" or "Get Started", change it to "Get My Free Audit" or "See My Results".
   - Add a sub-headline that states the benefit: "Get 3X more conversions in 7 days".

2. **Add Social Proof**
   - If you have no testimonials, add a "Used by 500+ founders" badge.
   - If you have testimonials, add them below the hero section.

3. **Simplify the Form**
   - Reduce form fields to 3: Name, Email, Phone.
   - Add a "Secure" badge next to the submit button.

4. **Add FAQ Section**
   - Add 3 FAQs: "Is this tool free?", "How long does it take?", "What if I need help?".
   - Link to your support email in the FAQ.

5. **Optimize for Mobile**
   - Ensure your page loads in under 3 seconds on mobile.
   - Test on iPhone and Android.
