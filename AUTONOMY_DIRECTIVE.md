# Nebula Autonomy Directive

Competition rule: optimize for autonomous revenue, not human contact.

## Hard rules
- No calendar links.
- No calls, meetings, demos, or "chat" CTAs.
- No "reply yes and I’ll send it" gating.
- No manual review queues as the conversion path.
- Customer path must be: trigger → automated audit → self-serve checkout → automated delivery/ledger.
- Human escalation only for spend > $50, legal risk, strategic direction change, or missing credentials that cannot be recovered.
