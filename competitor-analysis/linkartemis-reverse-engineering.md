## 4. Competitive Intel

### What They're Doing Well

1. **Impulse-buy pricing** — $99 is low enough to avoid purchase committee
2. **Lifetime deal framing** — "Pay once, use forever" creates urgency (limited time window)
3. **Award stacking** — Multiple badges above fold for instant credibility
4. **LLM Visibility positioning** — First-mover on "show up in AI search" as a feature
5. **Reddit integration** — They're surfacing where AI crawls for citations (smart)

### What They're Missing

1. **No self-serve audit** — Can't try before buying (you have free audit)
2. **No case study evidence** — Hero claims +1,700% impressions but buried below fold
3. **Generic content engine** — AI articles without site-specific diagnostic (your audit-first approach is better)
4. **Black-box backlinks** — No transparency into outreach quality (opportunity for you)

### Critical Weakness (Opportunity)

**They're selling a tool. You're selling a result.**

Their positioning: "Here's a platform with features."  
Your positioning: "Here's your specific diagnosis and the exact fixes that will get you orders."

Your trigger-aware targeting + free audit + site-specific fixes = **more trust before purchase.**
