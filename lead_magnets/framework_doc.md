# Conversion Funnel Framework

## Phase 1: Awareness
- **Goal**: Attract attention to problem
- **Tactics**: Educational content, problem-focused headlines, social proof
- **Metric**: Traffic volume, bounce rate

## Phase 2: Interest
- **Goal**: Demonstrate you understand their pain
- **Tactics**: Case studies, testimonials, problem-agitation copy
- **Metric**: Time on page, scroll depth

## Phase 3: Decision
- **Goal**: Build trust and credibility
- **Tactics**: Comparisons, guarantees, detailed explanations
- **Metric**: Conversion rate, form completions

## Phase 4: Action
- **Goal**: Remove friction to conversion
- **Tactics**: Clear CTAs, simplified forms, urgency elements
- **Metric**: Conversion rate, cart abandonment rate

## Phase 5: Retention
- **Goal**: Create loyal customers
- **Tactics**: Onboarding sequences, follow-up content, loyalty programs
- **Metric**: Repeat purchase rate, customer lifetime value

## Key Conversion Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** - Make your offer immediately understandable
2. **Benefit-focused language** - Focus on outcomes, not features
3. **Social proof** - People trust what others have experienced
4. **Urgency and scarcity** - Limited time/quantity increases action
5. **Risk reversal** - Guarantees reduce perceived risk
6. **Friction elimination** - Remove unnecessary steps from your funnel
7. **Mobile optimization** - Most traffic comes from mobile devices
8. **Load speed** - Every second of load time costs conversions

## Common Conversion Killers
- Confusing messaging
- Too many options
- Weak or unclear CTAs
- Lack of trust signals
- Slow load times
- Poor mobile experience
- No clear value proposition
