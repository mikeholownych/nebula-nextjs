## Positioning

Most founders do not have an ad problem first.

They have a destination problem.

Paid clicks are being pushed into a page that fails to capture trust, intent, or action. That is lead leakage. The fix is not “more traffic.” The fix is finding the leak before the next dollar goes into ads.

- Why clicks without sales usually means the page is the bottleneck.

- The promise in the ad must match the promise above the fold.
- If the ad sells a specific pain and the page opens generic, trust drops instantly.

- Most pages leak because they ask for too many actions.
- One visitor. One intent. One next step.
