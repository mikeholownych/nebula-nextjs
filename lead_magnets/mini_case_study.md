# Mini Case Study: $97 Landing Page Audit → $1.5k SDR Service

## Client Background
- SaaS startup with $50k/month ad spend
- Zero conversions from landing pages
- Frustrated founder actively searching for solutions

## Problem Identification
- Hero section lacked clear value proposition
- No social proof visible on landing page
- CTA buttons were unclear and buried
- Mobile experience was broken
- Page load time exceeded 5 seconds

## Solutions Implemented
1. **Hero Section Redesign**
   - Clear benefit-focused headline
   - Strong CTA button with action-oriented text
   - Social proof/testimonial added above fold

2. **Mobile Optimization**
   - Responsive design implemented
   - Font sizes adjusted for readability
   - CTAs made thumb-friendly

3. **Load Speed Improvement**
   - Images optimized
   - Unnecessary scripts minimized
   - Page load time reduced to 2.1 seconds

4. **Conversion Element Enhancement**
   - Form fields minimized
   - Security indicators added
   - Clear privacy policy link included

## Results
- **Conversion rate increased by 327%**
- **Cost per acquisition decreased by 68%**
- **Monthly revenue increased by $28,000**
- **Client upgraded to $1.5k/month SDR service**

## Key Takeaways
- Even small changes can have massive impact on conversions
- Mobile optimization is non-negotiable
- Load speed directly impacts conversion rates
- Clear CTAs and social proof are essential
- The client's pain point was clearly identified and addressed
