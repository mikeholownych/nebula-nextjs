## Sender Setup (Critical)

### From Line
```
Mike H from Nebula
```
NOT "Nebula Components" — human names outperform company names.

### Domain Warm-up
- Send from a warmed domain (not a fresh spam trap)
- Complete Google Workspace profile with photo
- DKIM + SPF + DMARC authentication

### Timing Windows

| Trigger Event | Timing | Action |
|---------------|--------|--------|
| LinkedIn post about ad spend pain | 0–24 hours | Comment first, then email |
| New ad campaign detected (RB2B) | 0–48 hours | "Saw your new campaign" angle |
| Competitor raised prices | Ongoing | Comparison angle |
| ICP posted about "traffic but no sales" | 0–6 hours | Direct reply to post |
