10 common questions

Will this work if I'm not running paid ads yet?
Yes. Organic traffic from SEO, Reddit, LinkedIn, or email has the same problem: if the page doesn't convert, the traffic is wasted. Fixing the conversion rate before scaling traffic is the highest-leverage move you can make — whether you're spending on ads or not.
What platforms do you support?
We audit and fix any landing page: Webflow, Squarespace, Framer, WordPress, Wix, custom-coded HTML/CSS, Shopify product pages, and more. The Fix Pack delivers assets you can implement regardless of platform — we'll note any platform-specific steps.
What if my page only scores a 7 or above?
If every dimension scores 7 or higher, we'll tell you that — and refund your $97. We'd rather give you your money back than deliver a fix pack that doesn't add value. That said, most pages score below 7 in at least one dimension that's actually costing conversions.
Do I need to give you access to my site or codebase?
No. We audit and build fixes from the public URL. The Fix Pack is a set of deliverables — copy, assets, code snippets — that you (or your developer) apply. Nothing is pushed directly to your production environment without your explicit action.
How is this different from running it through a free tool like PageSpeed Insights?
PageSpeed Insights tells you what's slow. It doesn't tell you why your headline isn't connecting, why your CTA is getting ignored, or why visitors are scrolling past your social proof. We look at the conversion psychology of the page — not just technical metrics — and we give you the fix, not just the score.
Is there a call involved?
No call. No calendar. No Zoom. You complete the intake form (URL + one sentence about your goal), we do the work, you receive the Fix Pack. If you have a question, email is fine. We designed this to be asynchronous so you don't have to block 45 minutes for a sales call.
What does "delivered in 24 hours" actually mean?
Within 24 hours of receiving your intake form, you receive: a scored audit report, a Fix Pack with implementation-ready assets, and a plain-English explanation of what was broken and why. If we're delayed (rare), we'll tell you proactively — not make you chase us.
What's the money-back guarantee exactly?
If your audit score doesn't show at least one fixable dimension under 7 — meaning we genuinely can't find anything worth fixing — we refund $97, no questions. We also stand behind the quality of the work: if the Fix Pack isn't implementable as delivered, we'll revise it.
Can you fix my whole website, not just one page?
The $97 Fix Pack covers one landing page. If you want multi-page coverage or a full conversion strategy, reach out after your first Fix Pack — we have a higher-tier option for that. But start with one page. Most of the conversion lift comes from fixing the single page your traffic hits first.
I already had an agency audit my site. Why would this be different?
Agency audits produce decks. We produce Fix Packs. A 40-slide PDF you pay $5k for doesn't fix your CTA button — it just tells you to think about fixing it. We skip the deck and go straight to the implementation. If you've got an agency report sitting unimplemented, we'll turn it into done work for $97.
