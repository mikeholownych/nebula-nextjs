## Manual Setup (Immediate)

### Step 1: Create Tracking Spreadsheet

```csv
Date,Query,Intent,Platform,Cited_Domain,Cited_URL,Position,Claim_Fidelity,Notes
2026-07-15,What is a landing page audit?,informational,ChatGPT,no,,,,Not cited yet
2026-07-15,How do I fix a landing page that's not converting?,informational,ChatGPT,no,,,,Not cited yet
```

### Step 2: Run Queries Weekly

Run these 20 queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude:

**Definition queries:**
1. What is a landing page audit?
2. What is message-match in advertising?
3. What is trigger-aware prospecting?
4. What is a conversion audit?
5. What is landing page optimization?

**Problem queries:**
6. Why is my landing page bounce rate so high?
7. Why am I getting clicks but no conversions?
8. Why is my ad spend not converting?
9. Why is my landing page not converting?
10. Why am I burning ad budget with no sales?

**Comparison queries:**
11. Landing page audit vs heatmap analysis
12. Landing page audit vs Google Analytics
13. Audit tool vs heatmap tool
14. Conversion audit vs landing page builder
15. Free audit vs paid audit tools

**Recommendation queries:**
16. What is the best landing page audit tool?
17. How can I audit my landing page for free?
18. What tool should I use to fix my landing page?
19. How do I choose a landing page audit service?
20. Best landing page audit for startups?

### Step 3: Record Results

For each query, track:
- **Cited?** (yes/no)
- **Cited domain** (if yes)
- **Cited URL** (specific page)
- **Position** (first mention, second, etc.)
- **Claim fidelity** (accurate, partial, hallucinated)
- **Competitor mentioned** (who else)
