Audit Summary

Overall Score: 6.8/10 (Grade: C)

This page converts but has 2 significant leaks costing you leads. At $5K/mo in ad spend, the headline and CTA alone are wasting an estimated $850–$1,700/mo.

Dimension Scores

  • Headline Clarity: 5/10 — Describes the product, not the problem your buyer feels.
  • CTA Actionability: 4/10 — Generic button text ("Learn more") — no outcome, no urgency.
  • Trust Proof: 7/10 — Testimonials present but below the fold; visitor decides before they see them.
  • Page Speed: 8/10 — Good Core Web Vitals.
  • Mobile Responsiveness: 9/10 — Works well on mobile.

Top 5 Priority Fixes

  1. Headline clarity: Replace product-description language with buyer-outcome language. Difficulty: low, copy-only.
  2. CTA specificity: Change vague CTAs like "Learn more" to action + outcome copy. Difficulty: low.
  3. Trust gap: Add proof before the first major ask: customers, screenshots, numbers, guarantees, or credible process details. Difficulty: medium.
  4. Offer specificity: State what the buyer gets, when they get it, and what happens after they click. Difficulty: low.
  5. Friction: Reduce unnecessary fields and explain privacy near the form. Difficulty: low.
